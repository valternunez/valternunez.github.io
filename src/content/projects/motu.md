Full-stack, built for one person or a shared household. Markdown notes link with `[[wiki-links]]` that resolve by
title (so they survive renames) and render as a force-directed knowledge graph; finances model
accounts, categorized transactions, installment plans, and computed card invoices; studies ship a
Pomodoro timer and weekly hour goals. Built to be lived in. *(Private repo; described, not shared.)*
